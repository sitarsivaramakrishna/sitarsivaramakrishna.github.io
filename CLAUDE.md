### Family & Training

- Disciple of the illustrious **Ustad Ahmed Hussain Khan** of **Senia Gharana** (Mian Achpal Gharana)

### Guru Lineage (Gharana)
- **Mian Achpal Gharana** — ancient and famous, great contributions to Hindustani Classical Music
- Ustad Ahmed Hussain Khan → trained by his father Ustad Bundu Khan Saheb, then under Ustad Rahimat Khan Saheb & Ustad Habib Khan Sahib
- Became an exponent of special techniques: Raza Khani, Maseet Khani, Seni Bhaj
- From Pune, moved to Madras at young age; teaching faculty at Bharatiya Vidya Bhavan, Madras
- **Rahimat Khan (1863-1954)**: Born to Ghulam Hussain Khan (vocalist, court of Bhavanagar). Grandfather Madaar Baksh was famous singer and sitar player in court of Ratlam. Trained under father, grandfather, and legendary beenkar Bande Ali Khan. Contemporary of Bhatkande and Paluskar. Founded 8 music schools. Settled in Dharwad in 1912. Credited with adding kharaj-shadaj string to sitar — inventing the kharaj-pancham-string setup widely used today.

## Reference Website Analysis (March 2026)

### sneheshnag.com
- Clean, hierarchical navigation: Home, Music (sub-sections), Gallery, Contact
- Warm color palette: cream/white backgrounds, warm oranges (#EA4322), soft pinks
- Serif fonts (Fraunces, Rufina) for classical elegance + sans-serif (Inter) for UI
- High-quality hero imagery, structured event listings, embedded contact form
- Restrained approach — no autoplay, good whitespace, accessibility-focused

### sitarsoumitra.com
- Built on Wix platform
- Coordinated color palette, Arial/Helvetica fonts
- Video integration (Vimeo), interactive buttons, responsive mesh-based layout
- Smooth page transitions (slide/fade), layered background system
- Modern multimedia portfolio optimized for content presentation

### purbayan.com
- Navigation: About, Albums, Our Bands, PAAMF, Events, News/Media
- Striking pink (#FF3773) accents on light gray (#F9F9F9) backgrounds
- Poppins font throughout for contemporary feel
- Streaming platform links prominently displayed (Spotify, Apple, Gaana, YouTube Music)
- Album artwork gallery, events with ticket booking, testimonials carousel
- Multiple bands presented with separate imagery/descriptions
- PAAMF Foundation section for educational initiatives
- News & Media integration with press coverage

### Common Patterns Across Reference Sites
1. Hero section with striking artist imagery
2. Prominent streaming platform links with icons
3. Event/concert listings with dates and venues
4. Photo galleries with professional photography
5. Video integration (YouTube/Vimeo embeds)
6. Clean, elegant typography mixing serif + sans-serif
7. Warm or bold accent colors against neutral backgrounds
8. Responsive design (mobile-friendly)
9. Social media links in footer
10. Press/media section for credibility
11. Multiple musical projects/bands get separate sections
12. Contact form for inquiries
